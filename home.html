SAVING MADE SIMPLE

Always wanted to explore the world, but never had the money to do so? Use Flying Pig. Sign up, connect your savings account, and start spending. Every time you purchase something, you also reroute some funds to your account. It's saving made simple.

TRAVEL

Have trouble saving money for a vacation? Whenever you make a purchase, round the amount spent up to the nearest dollar. The excess goes straight to your savings account. Set a goal so you can keep track of your progress.

CHARITY

Add your favorite charitable organization to send a small donation their way every time you make a purchase. Keep track of how much you've sent to each organization. Redirect your funds to another charity whenever you please.
